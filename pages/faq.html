Frequently Asked Questions

You've got Questions! We've got Answers!

How long do I have to cancel or reschedule my appointment?

We understand that plans can change! You can cancel or reschedule your appointment up to 24 hours in advance without any fees. if you cancel with 24 hours, a late cancellation fee may apply.

How early should i arrive for my appointment?

We recommend arriving 5-10 minutes before your appointment to allow time for check-in and a brief consultation with your stylist.

Do you accept walk-ins?

Yes, we accept walk-ins when possible! However, to ensure availability, we recommend booking an appointment in advance.

Do you offer consultations before booking a service?

Yes, we offer free consultations! This allows you to discuss your hair goals and oprions with a stylist to ensure you're booked for the right service.

Do you offer any discounts or promotions?

We regularly offer seasonal promotions and discounts for new clients, referrals, and certain services.

Can I have a specific stylist?

Absolutely! if you have a preferred stylist, you can request them when you book your appointment. We'll do our best to accommodate your request.
